Commercial Cleaning

Commercial Office Buildings Cleaning

Keep your workplace clean, organised, and professional with our tailored office cleaning services. A clean office boosts employee productivity and leaves a positive impression on clients.
Typical tasks include:
 • Dust and wipe desks, chairs, and common areas
 • Empty and sanitise rubbish bins
 • Vacuum and mop floors (scrub )
 • Clean and disinfect kitchenettes and toilets
 • Sanitise high-touch surfaces (e.g., door handles, phones)

Hospitality and Restaurant Cleaning

First impressions matter in hospitality. We provide detailed cleaning services for restaurants, cafes, and hotels to maintain a spotless, welcoming environment for guests and staff.
Typical tasks include:
 • Sanitise tables, chairs, and dining areas
 • Sweep, vacuum and mop all flooring/ carpet
 • Clean restrooms to hospitality standards
 • Waste removal and bin liner replacement
 • Clean toilets, change toilets papers, and refill handwash
 • Clean windows and doors
 • Remove cobwebs and blowing out areas

Medical Facilities Cleaning

Medical centres demand the highest cleaning standards to ensure a safe and hygienic environment. We follow strict cleaning protocols suited for healthcare settings.
Typical tasks include:
 • Disinfection of treatment rooms, waiting areas, and equipment
 • Cleaning and sanitising bathrooms and wash stations
 • Waste disposal, including clinical waste if required
 • Dusting surfaces and air vents
 • Floor cleaning using medical-grade disinfectants

Schools and Universities Cleaning

A clean educational environment encourages better learning outcomes. We offer flexible cleaning solutions for schools, colleges, and universities, customised to term times and schedules.
Typical tasks include:
 • Clean classrooms, lecture halls, windows and libraries
 • Empty bins and recycling
 • Disinfect desks, chairs, and shared equipment
 • Sanitize bathrooms and common areas
 • Mop and vacuum hallways and corridors
 • Clean toilets and change the toilets papers

Child Care and Day Care Cleaning

Safety and hygiene are critical in childcare facilities. We use child-safe, non-toxic products to ensure a healthy environment for young children.
Typical tasks include:
 • Clean and disinfect play areas, toys, windows and sleeping mats
 • Sanitise bathrooms, nappy change areas, and kitchens
 • Vacuum and mop all floors
 • Wipe down high-touch surfaces like door handles and tables
 • Empty and sanitise rubbish bins
 • Clean toilets and change the toilets papers

Gym and Fitness Center Cleaning

Gyms are high-traffic, high-contact environments that require regular disinfection. We help maintain a fresh, hygienic, and motivating space for members.
Typical tasks include:
 • Clean and disinfect gym equipment
 • Wipe down lockers, benches, and mirrors
 • Vacuum and mop exercise areas
 • Sanitise bathrooms, showers, and saunas
 • Refill soap and paper towel dispensers

Banks and Financial Institutions Cleaning

Banks and financial institutions require a clean and professional appearance to build trust with clients. We focus on detailed and discreet cleaning for such high-importance spaces.
Typical tasks include:
 • Dust and wipe desks, teller stations, and counters
 • Clean and sanitise ATMs and lobby areas
 • Floor vacuuming and mopping
 • Restroom cleaning and supply replenishment
 • Rubbish removal and bin sanitation
 • Clean toilets and change the toilets papers
